Italian Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Real Italian pasta. (Important for the emulsification), 100g per person
  • Guanciale, 50g per person
  • Pecorino Romano 25g per person
  • Egg Yolk, 1 per person
  • Whole Egg, 1
  • Empty mug to store pasta water
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

Prep Ingredients:

  1. Cut Guanciale into thin strips, with meat in middle and fat atop.
  2. Grate the Pecorino Romano as small as you can
  3. Seperate yolk(s) from whites and put into bowl. (Do with whites as you will)
  4. Add the 1 whole egg into bowl.
  5. Add generous amount of Black Pepper
  6. Beat with fork
  7. Start gradually mixing in the Pecorino and set aside

Cooking the meal:

  1. Put the Guanciale into a cold pan and put heat to medium-low and slowly render out the fat.
  2. Once the Guanciale is cooked, put aside leaving the rendered fat in the pan.
  3. Cook your pasta, just before finishing remove at least one mug full of pasta water.
  4. Place the Pasta into the pan and mix until the pasta absorbs all the oil (make sure you reheated pan slightly before this).
  5. Add a small amount of guanciale to the pasta.
  6. Pour a tiny amount of pasta water, just to cover the bottom into the pan.
  7. Bring the water you cooked the pasta back to a boil and place the pan with pasta atop of it.
  8. Add your egg mix and immediately start stirring.
  9. Add pasta water depending on whether or not you can toss the pasta in the pan.
  10. Plate and serve immediately with the rest of the Guanciale ontop, some more grated Pecorino and another drizzle of black pepper.
