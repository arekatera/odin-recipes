Garlic-Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 250g Chicken Breast
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Chicken Stock
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps:

Prep Ingredients:

  1. Cut / Butterfly breast if need.
  2. Salt + Pepper both sides of chicken breast.
  3. Mince garlic

Cooking the meal:

  1. Cook breast in butter till golden brown on both sides.
  2. Remove breast from pan.
  3. Add quarter cup of chicken stock and using a wooden spoon scrape the fond off the bottom.
  4. Add butter, thyme and garlic.
  5. Add chicken back in and baste for a couple minutes.
  6. Place chicken atop of rice
  7. Optional: Thicken sauce with a cornstarch slurry. (2tsp cornstarch + 2tbps water)
  8. Pour sauce over plated chicken and serve.
